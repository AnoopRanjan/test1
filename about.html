About OIC

Genius People. Quick Solutions.

OIC is a project-based engineering firm focused on software development and control systems. We develop and implement solutions for a wide range of industries using a variety of technologies and platforms. Since 1996, OIC has succeeded in helping hundreds of clients increase efficiency and productivity by delivering world-class solutions worldwide.

Our Services

OIC excels at helping clients automate processes. We are a technology and services company that provides software development, engineering, and control system solutions. Our goal is to increase the efficiency and productivity of your business with world-class solutions.

  • Manufacturing Automation & Intelligence
  • Test & Measurement Automation
  • Custom Software & Hardware Development
  • Office 365

Diverse Experience

Our diverse experience with technology, industry and business processes allows us to leverage best practices from many fields. We have a proven track record in a wide range of areas and industries.

Collaborative Approach

Think of our engineers as an extension of your team. OIC’s flexible and collaborative approach helps our clients solve technical challenges by listening to their needs and developing custom solutions. We’re comfortable working alongside your team and training them on the testing technology and details of the system delivered.

Locations

OIC offers engineering services from our offices in Chicago, Illinois; Boston, Massachusetts; Denver, Colorado; Houston, Texas; New York, New York and St. Louis, Missouri to customers worldwide.
