At OIC, we work on problems that few people in the country can solve. Firms come to us when they need advanced expertise to solve difficult problems. OIC isn’t a huge company where you’ll be working your way up for years and years. You’ll hit the ground running with your first project the week you arrive. OIC Perks

Variety

We’re a project-based company, giving you a wide variety of project sizes, industries, and technologies. You won’t be stuck on one project for years.

Customer Interaction

Every employee at OIC interfaces with customers, giving you the opportunity to develop sales and project management skills.

New Technologies

OIC is known for staying on the cutting edge of manufacturing-related technology. You’ll work with the latest innovations in nanotech, robotics, motion control, machine vision systems, and more.

Benefits

Full-time employees enjoy a great benefits package including health, dental, and vision coverage as well as 401K matching, generous paid vacation time, and a well-stocked kitchen full of snacks.

Events

We maintain an active company culture, where many of us bike or walk to work and participate in company-sponsored athletic, cultural, and social events.

Culture

OIC has been recognized as a “Best Place to Work.” We’re proud of our collaborative, team environment and mentorship program.

Locations

OIC serves customers worldwide from our office locations.
